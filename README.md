I'm a licensed radiographer from the Philippines trying my hand at web development. I have a passion for learning and sharing my knowledge with others in a way that's as public as possible. I love designing and creating things and recently, figuring out how websites and apps work has really piqued my interest.

I started early this year with HTML and CSS, making simple static webpages and expanding my knowledge over time. I then moved on to Javascript to create more dynamic websites. During this time, I also learned React to broaden my knowledge and skill to make web apps as well. I was recently picked up Ruby and Ruby on Rails as I try to extend my understanding on the backend stuff.

When I'm not developing and coding on my free time, you can find me watching Netflix, listening to music, trying to learn a new language, or playing with my cats. I also plan on creating blog posts about everything I've learned so far. Hopefully, you'll see a list of what I am up to on the website I'm planning to develop some time in the future.

I'm really curious where this journey will lead me.
